Why Target Space

Target Space is Kenya's Leading Workplace Media Communication Specialist.

We like to see ourselves as the pep in the ad-world’s step.

Welcome to Target Space, Kenya’s leading workplace media communication specialist. We like to see ourselves as the pep in the ad-world’s step. Everyone can advertise with anyone, almost anywhere. But we found there was little to no control or insight in the realm of OOH. That’s where we come in, we help connect brands to high-value captive audiences, with ease, effectiveness and analytics, in buildings across Kenya’s Capital. With a focus on technology, we leverage programmatic content to provide our advertising clients with captive bespoke brand moments that raise awareness, spark interest and engage with audiences. Our team of creatives, programmatic specialists and media consultants help our clients build brands, speak to their audiences and gain loyalty through relevant and thoughtful communication. We strive to help businesses transform anytime into prime time for advertising and impact ready-to-spend urban residents with our advertisements, while enabling property managers’ communication with their residents. We focus on the high traffic areas in front of the elevators.

Our Story

How it All Begun.

Many years ago, our founder Joseph Onoka went to one of the Capitals office building to seek for services, after which he later commuted to a different building in the outskirts of the CBD, little did he know the same service was on different floor in the same building he visited and this sparked the idea to create a company to improve how buildings communicate with tenants and visitors. This unexplored frontier represented a tremendous opportunity and we seized it.
Since Late 2020, Target Space has been laying the foundation to help small- and medium-sized businesses to powerfully and affordably multiply the reach of their advertisements. Solid tracks were laid down and ground breaking spikes were hammered into place to develop patent-pending technology to provide our clients with the ultimate micro-targeted communication channel optimizing the impact of every media shilling spent. Who knew that the elevator presented such a uniquely captivating space?.
Today the frontier was crossed. Now there is a bridge that connects the two worlds, small business can affordably access our elevator lobby-based TV advertising and reach very targeted consumer segments on over 5 screens and counting.
